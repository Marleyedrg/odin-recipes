chips

    Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt

    • Preparation method:

  • Slice the potatoes very thinly using a mandoline or sharp knife.
  • Soak the potato slices in cold water for at least 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  • Dry the potato slices thoroughly with a clean kitchen towel or paper towel.
  • In a large pot or deep fryer, heat the vegetable oil to 180°C/350°F.
  • Carefully add a small batch of potato slices to the hot oil and fry for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown and crispy.
  • Remove the chips from the oil with a slotted spoon and place them on a plate lined with paper towels to remove excess oil.
  • Sprinkle salt over the chips while they are still hot and serve immediately.
