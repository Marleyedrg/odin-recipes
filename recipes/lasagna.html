Lasagna

    Ingredients:

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef (optional)
  • Grated cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • Spices (oregano, basil, garlic, etc.)

    • Preparation method:

  • Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.
  • In a pan, cook the ground beef (if using) and add desired spices.
  • In a casserole dish, add a thin layer of tomato sauce on the bottom.
  • Then, add a layer of lasagna pasta, followed by a layer of ground beef, and a layer of grated cheese.
  • Repeat the layers until the dish is filled, ending with a layer of grated cheese.
  • Cover with aluminum foil and bake for about 25 minutes.
  • Remove the aluminum foil and let it for another 10 minutes for grating.
  • Serve hot.
